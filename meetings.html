Support Savannah AA

Celebrating an AA birthday? Consider donating $1, $2 or even $5 a year for each year ‒– or a penny or a nickel a day for each day of your sobriety.

Donations of any size welcome. Contributions support members and groups in Chatham, Bryant & Effingham County, and help ensure that the A.A. message is carried to newcomers. Because our traditions state that we be fully self-supporting, we ask that only AA members donate.
